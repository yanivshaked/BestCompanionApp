Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

BestCompanion is a calendar display application designed to help elderly individuals and people living with dementia, Alzheimer's disease, or age-related memory challenges. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

Information We Access

BestCompanion accesses the following data through your Google account:

  • Google Calendar events — The app reads your calendar events for the current day in order to display them on screen. This includes event titles, times, and calendar names.
  • Google account email — Used solely for authentication through Google Sign-In.

How We Use Your Information

  • Calendar events are fetched and displayed on the device screen in real time.
  • Your Google account email is used only to authenticate with Google Calendar.
  • No data is used for advertising, analytics, profiling, or any purpose other than displaying your calendar.

Data Storage

  • BestCompanion does not store your calendar events or personal data on any external server.
  • All calendar data is fetched directly from Google's servers and kept in the device's temporary memory only while the app is running.
  • User preferences (display settings, language, selected calendars) are stored locally on the device using standard Android storage and are never transmitted.

Data Sharing

BestCompanion does not share, sell, or transmit your personal data or calendar information to any third party. The only network communication is directly between the app and Google's Calendar API on your behalf.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In — For account authentication. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Calendar API — For reading calendar events. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Permissions

  • Internet access — Required to connect to Google Calendar and fetch events.
  • Calendar read access — Requested via Google Sign-In OAuth2 scope (calendar.readonly). The app can only read calendar data; it cannot create, modify, or delete events.

Children's Privacy

BestCompanion is not directed at children under the age of 13. The app is designed as a caregiver tool for elderly individuals. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Retention and Deletion

  • Calendar data exists only in temporary memory while the app is running and is cleared when the app is closed.
  • To remove all locally stored preferences, you can clear the app's data in Android Settings or uninstall the app.
  • To revoke calendar access, remove BestCompanion from your Google account's connected apps at myaccount.google.com/permissions.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
